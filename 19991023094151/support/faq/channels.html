Channels

How do I join a channel?

Type /join #channel into your client.

How do I see a list of channels that I can join?

Type /list. Beware that the channels list is very long and may take some time to fetch.

How do I leave a channel?

Type /part #channel.

How do I start a channel of my own?

If you join a channel that doesn't exist, it is created for you and you are made the channel operator.

What is a channel operator?

Channel operators ("ops") manage a channel. They can modify the way the channel behaves, remove users from the channel and make other users into ops. Channel operators have the @ symbol beside their nicknames.

How do I change the topic in a channel?

Use /topic #channel text. If the channel has a mode of +t set (most do) only ops can change the topic.

How do I change a channel's modes?

/mode #channel +modes. You need ops to change modes.

How do I give a user ops?

Use the mode change /mode #channel +o nick . To take ops away use the -o mode.

How do I kick a user from a channel?

/kick #channel nick [reason]. You need ops to kick.

What do the different channel modes mean? 

 Modes  m                 moderated (+o and +v talk only)
        i                 invite only
        s                 secret (won't show on list)
        p                 private (won't show on list or /whois)
        n                 must be in channel to send message
        t                 only ops may change topic
        b [banmask]       ban management
        l [limit number]  limit channel number
        o [nickname]      give/remove ops
        v [nickname]      give/remove voice
        k [key]           add/remove channel key
