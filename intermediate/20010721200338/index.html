WELCOME TO AUSTNET!

 AustNet is an internet chat network. Our servers are located in Canada, New Zealand, the United States, Australia, Europe and Singapore.

We base our network around the principles of providing our community with the best servers, services and support possible.		 ABOUT US

 AustNet is governed by a charter, and is run by a group of volunteers around the world.  If you have any questions, please feel free to email our support mailing list help@austnet.org.

If you are a new user, you can follow our get connected guide, alternatively you can jump online through the web!
