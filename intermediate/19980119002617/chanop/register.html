-ChanOP- *** Command Help ***
-ChanOP- This service allows channel managers to maintain complete
-ChanOP- control of their registered channel. ChanOP offers a stable
-ChanOP- environment for channels by preventing malicious takeovers,
-ChanOP- while allowing operators the facility of a user database.
-ChanOP-  
-ChanOP- Registrations make you the channel manager of your channel.
-ChanOP- You should be familiar with users addition, deletion and
-ChanOP- channel configurations as part of your responsibility as
-ChanOP- a channel manager. Please note that registrations expire
-ChanOP- after two weeks of inactivity.
-ChanOP-  
-ChanOP- *** Command Syntax ***
-ChanOP-    Syntax: register <channel>
-ChanOP-    Access: No channel access required.
-ChanOP-  Examples:  /msg ChanOP register #chatzone
-ChanOP-                     (Registers #ChatZone to you)
-ChanOP-  See Also: DROP, INFO, ADDUSER, SET
