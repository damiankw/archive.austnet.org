Channel of the Week:  

  #Austscripters pride themselves on providing a high level of support to the
AustNet scripting and non scripting community. The channel does not have an official script, but work on a new script is starting shortly.  
As with any technical channels there are often critics who come in to try and "trip up" so to speak, the members of the channel by asking stupid questions
that they expect people will get wrong, `Eggman recalls one such time when a
long time AustNet scripter Cool1 tried to do this and ended up getting banned!
   #Austscripters likes to think of itself as "the place to go when nobody else knows/cares". If you have a scripting question, give them a try :)

#AustScripters  

Reported by Karl

#Austscripters is AustNet's oldest scripting help channel.
   Although the registration date states that #AustScripters was registered in
October of 2000, this author recalls going and op'ing in there as far back as 98. The channel has changed owners a number of times. Originally it was owned by asdhgsa, then PrOTeX, Travis/Tray, Nicolas and `Eggman who is the current owner.
  The name #Austscripters originated from the fact it was a scripting channel on an Australian network, although interestingly the majority of the owners did not live in Australia.
  `Eggman found himself the owner of the channel when the previous owner Nicolas couldn't give it the attention it needed. He's been a member of the channel since he first connected to AustNet, and thinks it's the best scripting channel on Austnet.
  The purpose of the channel is for programming/scripting discussion and help. People often drop by when they need help with mIRC scripts or other types of scripting. `Eggman says that discussion includes "anything that comes up that people are interested in" - including even such esoteric subjects as the basic format of the DNS protocol!
