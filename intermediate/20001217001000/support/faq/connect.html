Connecting

I can't connect. What should I do?

Mail help@austnet.org with:
  • The name of the server you were trying to connect to.
  • The program you were using to connect.
  • Your Internet Service Provider's name.
  • Your computer's address, if you can find it.
  • Any error/disconnect messages you received.
  • The time of the incident.
If you provide this you will get a speedy response. If you don't, you won't.
