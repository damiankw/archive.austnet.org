Website News

We intend to provide you with a helpful website that has all the information you need to start you on the AustNet IRC Network. If you feel we have missed something, let us know! You can email us at the Website mailing list.



AustNet is much more than a network of servers and the links that bind them. It is a network made up of people from all corners of the globe, that interact and share each other's lives. This part of the AustNet website will progressively become an interactive section that allows the community of AustNet to contribute to the website and let others know what is going on in their corner of the network.
