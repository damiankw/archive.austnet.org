Welcome to The Chameleon Tree
Connected April 15
AustNet welcomes The Chameleon Tree, Inc, located in San Diego, USA. san-diego.austnet.org closes a hole that existed in our USA connectivity. Easing the load on the other usa servers, san-diego makes a valuable addition to AustNet.

Welcome CyberWay
Connected March 13
AustNet welcomes Cyberway Internet, located in Singapore, to the network. With a dedicated direct link between Singapore, Japan and Australia, Cyberway offers Singapore clients a faster connection.
