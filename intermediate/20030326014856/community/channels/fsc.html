#FunkySexyCool - AustNet's latest pickup joint  

Reported by Ed

  #FunkySexyCool is quickly becoming AustNet’s premier hook-up channel, with a nightly fill of around 290.
   Founded in June 2002, #FunkySexyCool or ‘FSC’ as it’s known to it’s members has silenced it’s critics by becoming one of AustNet’s top 10 channels in less than a year.
  The channel’s owner, FLAVi_G (a.k.a. Flavs), attributes the channel’s success to the website which is an integral part of the profile-trading idea which has made the channel so popular. Users can add a profile of themselves to the website, which includes a picture and some information, and then paste the profiles URL in the channel, for others to view and vote on.
  I asked FLAVi_G what had made the channel so popular. “We get a lot of people putting their pics on the site because their friends told them about it or either they see someone they like they want to make a profile to msg them, also it's an easier way of showing people your pic, you're a paste of a link away.” he said. People wanting others to see and vote on their profile encourages others to check out the website, and maybe create their own account.
