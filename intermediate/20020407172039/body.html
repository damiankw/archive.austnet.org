 Welcome!
Welcome to AustNet - a global network with servers in all corners of the globe. To learn more about how to start chatting on the AustNet Internet Relay Chat Network, read our New User's Guide. While you're here, why not explore services which AustNet makes available to you for free?

 Virtual World
AustNet has released Virtual World, which makes life harder for those who participate in nuking and other denial of service attacks by masking your real IP address. By doing this, they cannot resolve your IP address, and therefore prevents you from being nuked. Visit the Virtual World site for more info.

 In The Loop
AustNet continues to grow - February 2002
Thanks to the clients who have chosen to make Austnet their home. We value client feedback, and look forward to constructive comments which you can submit here.

AustNet Anti-Spamming Measures - March, 2000
AustNet is implementing measures to reduce and ban clients who spam or mass advertise on the network. As the code matures, these anti-spam measures will be deployed on a larger scale.

New Security Implementation - January, 1999
AustNet is now beginning to implement patches to fight security problems introduced by WinGate. Should you find yourself banned from AustNet because of an "Insecured WinGate", you can find information on correcting this here.
