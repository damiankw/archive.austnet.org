-ChanOP- *** Command Help ***
-ChanOP- Suspend is used to temporarily remove a users access on ChanOP.
-ChanOP- Suspended users cannot access any commands until suspend expires
-ChanOP- or is removed.
-ChanOP-  
-ChanOP- *** Command Syntax ***
-ChanOP-    Syntax: setuser <#chan> <nick> suspend <off | number minutes>
-ChanOP-    Access: Requires (150) channel access.
-ChanOP-  Examples:  /msg ChanOP setuser #chat RogerY suspend 15
-ChanOP-                     (Will suspend RogerY on #chat for 15 minutes)
-ChanOP-                       /msg ChanOP setuser #chat RogerY suspend off
-ChanOP-                     (Will turn off RogerY's suspend)
-ChanOP-  See Also: ADDUSER, DELUSER, ACCESS
