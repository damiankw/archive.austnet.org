Sponsors!
Would you like to sponsor one of these competitions on the Austnet website? Our site gets approximately 13000 visitors each month - a great advertisement for your business or organisation, and a chance for you to give back to our Austnet community

Contact the Publicrelations Dept today!
   


What's eligible?

Original graphic buttons, static or animated (89 pixels wide x 31 pixels high) in .jpg or .gif format. Buttons must be suitable to use as a link to the main Austnet website.

Who can enter?

Registered users of the AustNet IRC Network may enter the competition.

Who are the judges?

"Aka": AustNet Director of Infrastructure;
"Hippychick": AustNet Director of Publicrelations, + multimedia developer;
"Maxmax6" - Competition sponsor and owner of Maxmax6 Professional Hosting Solutions.

How to enter:

Send your button(s) by email, along with your registered AustNet nickname, to competitions@austnet.org, with the subject "Button Design Competition", by 5pm AWST 2nd June, 2003.

Prize

The winner will receive one year's professional webhosting (m5 plan) as detailed on www.maxmax6.com/shared.php, as well as registration of a .com, .net or .org domain for one year. Total prize value is $140.00 AU. Prize was donated to AustNet by Maxmax6 Professional Hosting Solutions. The judges' decision is final, and no correspondence will be entered into.

Other rules

All work must be original.
Chance plays no part in the outcome of this competition.
Buttons will be judged based on originality, creativity, download time, visual impact, and technical proficiency.
All buttons entered become the property of the AustNet IRC Network and may be used to promote the AustNet website;
Maximum of 3 entries per person;
Entries submitted without a registered Austnet nickname will be discarded;
Entries close 5pm AWST 2nd June, 2003.
Ten (10) best entries will be displayed on the Austnet community website (http://community.austnet.org) from 9 am AWST 5th June, 2003. The winner will be announced on 12th June, 2003 on the main Austnet website.
