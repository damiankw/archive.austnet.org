Commands

How do I find a person given his nick?

/whois nick.

How do I find a person if I know his address or part of it?

/who *domainname* (read the question on addresses for what a domain name is) will sometimes find the person.

How do I set an away message? How do I cancel it?

/away reason to set and the same with no reason to clear.

Where can I find out how CTCP works?

Try the CTCP protocol guide.
