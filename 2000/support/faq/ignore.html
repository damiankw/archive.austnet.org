Why am I ignored by Austnet Services

  1. Your identd is part of how the irc server sees you when you connect identd@

  2. Many user scripts when downloaded and installed on your irc client change your identd value.

  3. Many users just type any name they think of for an identd

How to fix this problem

For users of mIRC you can use one of these two methods

 

1.

//identd on $me 

//server $server 

for the new identd to take effect. This option will only work when you are on IRC

 

2.

go to file-->setup-->identd and change what is written in userID , then reconnect to take effect .

For users of pIRCh you can use this method

1.

go to tools ---> ident server  and in the ident box put your userid then check the active box, then reconnect to take effect.
