Protection

How do I stop somebody flooding or abusing me?

Use the /ignore command on your client. All clients support /ignore *!*user@*.domainname (if you don't know how addresses work read that question). In BitchX use /ig nick which automatically calculates the address. In mIRC use /ignore nick 3.

How do I stop somebody nuking me?

Visit the Security page, download and install the patches that apply to your system.

What does "nick sets mode: +v" mean? What is VirtualWorld?

VirtualWorld hides your network address from other IRCers, which makes you very difficult to nuke. When you have usermode +v set, VirtualWorld is active. If you set yourself -v you lose this protection. For more information see the VirtualWorld page or the guidelines for effective VirtualWorld use.

NOTE NOTE NOTE: Usermode +v is NOT the same as having +v (voice; the little + sign) in a channel. Being voiced in a channel gives you NO protection from nukes.
