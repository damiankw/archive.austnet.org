Channel Meets
(29th March, 2003, Submitted by various)
Over the coming weeks several channels have meets planned, where their members can get together "in real life". AustNet will soon be including a new "events" section where events on the network will be announced, and channel meets given on a calendar.
#Albania - The organisers and owners of #albania have organised a meet this saturday at Bubble nightclub in Melbourne to celebrate SicK_aLbAnIaN's birthday. All users are keen and can't wait to go. (by Sick_Albanian)
#Picturepoll - PicturePoll is having its second BBQ at Albert park after it's first successful BBQ in 2001. More details are on the website HERE (by ravemasta)
#Footscray is planning a channel meet in the near future (more details when we known them) (by John_G)
#30&40's - The Adelaide social meet for #30&40's is being held on the 5th of April. Please attend and have some fun. People from other states are attending as well. Visit #30&40's for more info. (by Jacksta)

We are planning a new section of the Austnet website, with a calendar of channel meets. If you have a channel meet coming up, please let us know by emailing us with the channel name, your normal nickname on AustNet, date, place and time of the meeting (or where readers can get the information) and any other information.
 
Tech Discussions on Tuesdays
12th March, 2003, Submitted by Gramyre
#atomicmpc (official channel of Atomic MPC computing magazine) holds a tech discussion every Tuesday night at 8pm (AEST). The format is moderated and get in early to ask your tech questions on the theme of the week. For further information drop into #atomicmpc or visit the forums on www.atomicmpc.com.au
