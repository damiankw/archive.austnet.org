About #LoveOP and LoveOP:
#LoveOP is the support channel for AustNet's special service LoveOP. The people there can show you how to use LoveOP, answer any questions you have about him, or even arrange an IRC wedding for you and that special person.

LoveOP provides fun ways to show somebody that you care. If you're shy you can be the romantic secret admirer -- LoveOP can let the object of your affections know, without revealing your identity! You can ask LoveOP to send your love chocolates, roses, love notes and other gifts, and when things get really serious you can become a "netcouple", or share the ultimate bond of love with an IRC marriage.

Frequently asked questions:
Secret admirers will always remain secret, however much you're dying to know who your tall, dark stranger is. There is no way to make LoveOP break his vow of silence.

Commands to try:
Things you can send
/msg loveop admire [nick]/msg loveop hug [nick]
/msg loveop chocolate [nick]/msg loveop rose [nick]
/msg loveop candy [nick]/msg loveop cookie [nick]
Things you can do
Send a lovenote/msg LoveOP lovenote [nick] [cute message]
Send an apology/msg LoveOP apology [nick] [reason]
Give a kiss/msg LoveOP kiss [nick] [gentle or tonsil]
Vote/msg LoveOP vote [nick] [category you nominate the person for]
Netcouples
Become a netcouple/msg LoveOP netcouple add [partner]
Book a wedding/msg LoveOP book [you] [partner]
Break up/msg LoveOP netcouple del [ex-partner]
Yes, you can try most commands on yourself first :-)
