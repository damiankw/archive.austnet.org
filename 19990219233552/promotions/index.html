High Art
A story of AMBITION, SACRIFICE, SEDUCTION and other career moves.

At 24 Syd (Radha Mitchell) has landed what seems to be a dream job at the prestigious photography magazine FRAME, but despite a recent promotion and the long hours she works , she's still stuck fetching coffee. Living with her longtime boyfriend James (Gabriel Mann), Syd has reached a comfortable, if not particularly exciting, level of stability. One night while soaking in the tub after another long day at the office, Syd notices a leak in the ceiling. She ventures upstairs to investigate and a chance meeting suddenly takes her down a path she never expected.
