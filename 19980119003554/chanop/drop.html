-ChanOP- *** Command Help ***  
-ChanOP- This command is used to remove a channel from registration 
-ChanOP- and removes the entire channel database.  Thus, is only 
-ChanOP- able to be issued by the channel manager. 
-ChanOP-   
-ChanOP- *** Command Syntax ***  
-ChanOP-    Syntax: drop <channel> 
-ChanOP-    Access: Requires (200) channel access. 
-ChanOP-  Examples: /msg ChanOP drop #chat 
-ChanOP-                    (Removes #chat from registration) 
-ChanOP-  See Also: REGISTER, INFO
