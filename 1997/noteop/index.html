AUSTnet has worked hard to bring you the best of everything in the IRC world. We have designed from scratch 4 services to help with Network, nick, channel, and contact management. This page concentrates on NoteOp, the service which allows a user to send a note to another user when they are not online at the time. NoteOp was designed as a message carrier, if there is a channel meeting or if you just want to drop a note to a friend to say hello. If you have any questions reguarding NoteOp, feel free to join #Austop while on our network.

All NoteOp commands can be accessed on the network by typing /msg Nickop help
