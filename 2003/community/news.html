Do you have any news items for this section of the website? We want to hear from you.


Do you know some information that belongs on our news pages? Perhaps something that is happening in your channel, or something else that AustNet users should know about? Submit your news item using our form below. The categories of news are:
  Administration News:
Changes in administration - (Admin/IRC Operators/ASD), changes in the charter/agreement etc  
  Alerts:
Virus warnings, security alerts, warnings about password thieves and anything else of this nature.
  Channel News:
Channel news and events, changes in popular channels, meets
  Events:
upcoming events on AustNet in association with other organisations
  General Interest:
Such as project orca - anything that concerns austnet but is just generally interesting
  Network News:
Testlinked/permlinked/delinked servers, peaks for Austnet, upgrades and planned outages
  Services News:
changes to Austnet services (OPAS)
  Website News:
changes and additions to the site
Think about what people need to know about your subject (who, what, where, how and why), and try to make it as interesting as possible. eg: If it's about a channel meet, make sure you give details, if its open (anyone can come).
