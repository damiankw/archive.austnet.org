IRCops/Helpers

How can I become an IRC op?

  • Short Answer: You can't.
  • Long Answer: You either own a server, or are chosen to help run one. People are chosen for their knowledge and temperament as demonstrated on IRC, and not because they ask to be IRC ops; refusal often offends.

How can I become a helper?

By joining #help or #asd, and giving help with problems for which you know the correct solution. If you are seen to be a worthwhile helper you may be invited to join ASD.

How can I find an IRC op or an ASD admin when I want one?

/msg Sprint listadmin to find IRC operators and /msg ASD listadmin to find ASD administrators and helpers.
