We intend to provide you with a helpful website that has all the information you need to start you on the AustNet IRC Network. If you feel we have missed something, let us know! You can email us at the Website mailing list.



There are various methods of contacting AustNet based upon who the audience of your request should be. Simply click on the any of the following mail links to contact AustNet:

General Help and Support Requests
For assistance with any AustNet related problems or issues, you can mail the AustNet Support Department and the network will reply as soon as possible. Topics normally include connection details, service assistance and general network information.

Individual help requests should be mailed to help@austnet.org
Permanent subscription to the help@austnet.org mailing list should be done by sending a blank email to help-subscribe@austnet.org.

Services Department
The AustNet Services Department maintains a support service for the questions and discussion specifically related to AustNet services. This entails recommendations, enhancement notifications, channel and nickname registration disputes and/or mediation.

Individual help requests should be mailed to services@austnet.org
Permanent subscription to the services@austnet.org mailing list should be done by sending a blank email to services-subscribe@austnet.org.

Network Topology and Routing (Closed List)
The Routing Department discusses and manages AustNet's topology and server connectivity. It is responsible for server applications, maintenance and announcements.

Individual help requests should be simply mailed to routing@austnet.org.
This list is not available to the public for subscription.

Abuse Mediation
AustNet encourages users who may be concerned about the actions of official AustNet staff to e-mail the abuse response service. The board of will resolve any concerns or issues you may have in a fair and independant manner, making sure that AustNet remains one of the best IRC networks around.

Requests regarding IRCOps or Admin should be emailed to abuse@austnet.org
Requests regarding ASD helpers should be emailed to asdadmin@austnet.org.

Public Relations
The Public Relations department cooperatively organises network events on AustNet, as well as organising special channels for these events. In addition, the department also supervises the continuing development of the AustNet website, and supervises a news mailing list news@austnet.org.

To contact the Public Relations department (for example, to organise an event), email pr@austnet.org
A general discussion list on how to raise the profile of AustNet in a positive way is publicrelations@austnet.org. Permanent subscription to the list should be done by sending a blank email to publicrelations-subscribe@austnet.org.
To make suggestions for the website, email website@austnet.org
To subscribe to the news list, send a blank email to
news-subscribe@austnet.org

AustNet Development Mailing List
AustNet maintains an IRCd development team mailing list for discussion pertaining to the development of the server software. Comments and suggestions for modifications can also be sent to this list.

Individual suggestions/comments should be mailed to dev@austnet.org
Permanent subscription to the dev@austnet.org mailing list should be done by sending a blank email to dev-subscribe@austnet.org.
