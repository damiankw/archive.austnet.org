Miscellaneous

How can I find a user's address for a ban or ignore?

First you do a /whois lookup: /whois nick. The format of replies varies between clients, but you should get something similar to this:
*** Cork - nswillis@vw-9473.ozemail.com.au
This tells you:
  • The username: nswillis
  • The VirtualWorld account: vw-9473
  • The ISP/domain name: ozemail.com.au
You can assemble these pieces in various combinations. The useful ones are *!*nswillis@*.ozemail.com.au for more permanent use and *!*@vw-9473.ozemail.com.au when a strong temporary ban/ignore is needed.

What document shows me how IRC "really works"?

That would be RFC 1459.

How do I obtain somebody's real IP address over AustNet?

You can't. AustNet does not reveal a user's real IP address through server technology called Virtual World.
