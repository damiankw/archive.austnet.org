How do I unban myself/another person from outside a channel?

Use /msg chanop unban #channel nick. You must have enough access to remove the ban.

What do I do if I forget my password?

You need to visit #ASD and ask to have it mailed out to you. It will be mailed to the address you specified when you registered your nickname.

How do I register a nickname? How do I deregister one?

  • Registration: /msg nickop register password emailaddress registers your current nick. Consult the Password Guide before choosing a password and make sure you enter your real email address in case you forget your password.
  • Deregistration: /msg nickop drop deregisters your current nick.

How do I register a channel? How do I deregister one?

  • Registration: /msg chanop register #channel will activate registration. You must then read the instructions which ChanOP gives. To activate registration you must have ops in the channel.
  • Deregistration: /msg chanop drop #channel.

How do I let only certain levels change my channel's topic?

Use /msg chanop set #channel keeptopic minlevel.

How do I kill somebody using my nick? Can I make this happen automatically?

/msg nickop kill nickname password. The command /msg nickop set kill on will make this automatic for your current nick.
