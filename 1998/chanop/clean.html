-ChanOP- *** Command Help *** 
-ChanOP- The clean command is used to remove all modes on a 
-ChanOP- channel. These modes include: 
-ChanOP-   
-ChanOP- +k (key channels) +l (limits) +i (invite only) 
-ChanOP- +m (moderation)   +s (secret) +p (private) 
-ChanOP- +n (non-external) +t (topic restriction) 
-ChanOP-   
-ChanOP- As a server, ChanOP can remove these modes within or 
-ChanOP- outside the channel.  Ban and Op modes, however, are not  
-ChanOP- covered within the clean command, for details on removing 
-ChanOP- channel bans, /msg ChanOP help unban, and for removing 
-ChanOP- channel ops, /msg ChanOP help mdeop. 
-ChanOP-   
-ChanOP- *** Command Syntax *** 
-ChanOP-    Syntax: clean <channel> 
-ChanOP-    Access: Requires (100) channel access. 
-ChanOP-  Examples: /msg ChanOP clean #Sydney 
-ChanOP-                     (Cleans all channel modes on #Sydney) 
-ChanOP-  See Also: UNBAN, MDEOP
