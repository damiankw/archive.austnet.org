Contacting
austnet.org
There are various methods of contacting AUSTnet based upon who the audienceof your request should be. Simply click on the any of the following maillinks to contact AUSTnet:

Client Help Mailbox
    For assistance with any AUSTnet related problems or issues, you can mail a help mailbox and the network will reply as soon as possible. Topics normally include connection details, service assistance and general network information. It is estimated your post will be replied within one hour or less during normal business hours.

    mail to: help@austnet.org
    with the the body of your question/request
User's Mailing List
    The User Mailing List is an open public mailing list available for AUSTnet clients. This mailing list is the main discussion forum for the management of the network, user requests and general discussion.

    mail to: majordomo@austnet.org
    with the body: subscribe AUSTnet
Services Mailing List
    The services mailing list is for OPAS related questions and discussion. This entails recommendations, enhancement notifications, channel and nickname registration disputes or mediation.

    mail to: majordomo@austnet.org
    with the body: subscribe services
Online Assistance
    If you require online assistance, you can contact a network operator, or you can join any of the help channels:

    Operator assistance: /join #AUSTop General Assistance: /join #help
    Find an Operator: /who -op
    (mIRC users: /raw who 0 o)
